Terms of Service

1. The "Non-Commercial Beta" Status

Sarayu Iron (v0.1) is a private, experimental research project. By using a subdomain or VM, you acknowledge that this is not a commercial service. There are no uptime guarantees, and the 'provider' (Me) can terminate any instance for maintenance without notice.

2. The "Hardware Protection" (SRE Clause)

Users are strictly prohibited from running:

  • Crypto-mining/Hashing (This will trigger an auto-kill of your PID).
  • Stress-testing/Benchmarking without written permission.
  • Heavy constant IO that degrades NVMe lifespan.
3. The "12-Hour Micro-Blip" Rule

We follow the '12-Hour Threshold.' Any outage caused by ISP (Etisalat/du), Power (SEWA), or local hardware failure lasting less than 12 hours is considered 'Normal Operating Noise' and is not an incident.

4. The "Cybercrime Zero-Tolerance"

In accordance with UAE Federal Law No. 34 of 2021, any use of this infrastructure for:

  • Phishing/Scams
  • Spreading 'Rumors' or fake news
  • Accessing prohibited content
...will result in an immediate Permanent Ban and reporting to the relevant 'Cyber Pulse' initiatives.

Acceptable Use Policy

1. Hardware & Performance Constraints

To ensure the stability of the "Sarayu Iron" node, the following activities are strictly prohibited:

/

Crypto-mining: No hashing, mining, or validation of any PoW or PoS protocols.

/

Stress Testing: No stress-ng, prime95, or intensive benchmarks pegging all cores for more than 60 seconds without prior SRE approval.

/

BitTorrent: No hosting of seedboxes or public trackers that cause high-frequency disk I/O.

2. Legal Compliance (UAE Federal Law)
•

Public Morals: No hosting of gambling sites, pornographic material, or content that prejudices public morals.

•

National Security: No publishing of information that threatens state security or public order.

•

Privacy & Defamation: No "doxxing," spread of rumors, or fake news.

We monitor resource metrics in real-time. If your subdomain triggers a "Resource Spike," our automated watchdog will:

Service Level Agreement

1. Operational Availability

Sarayu targets 99.9% availability for the edge network layer. Outages under 12 hours caused by regional utilities or designated maintenance windows are considered 'Normal Operating Noise'.

2. Performance & Hardware Integrity

To maintain hardware health (NVMe lifespan and CPU stability), crypto-mining and unauthorized stress testing are strictly prohibited. The 'SRE Kill-Switch' may be deployed if resource usage threatens shared node stability.

3. Data Privacy & Sovereignty

We follow a Zero-Tracking philosophy. Access logs are purged every 60 days. We do not use hosted data to train AI/LLM models. All infrastructure resides within the UAE.

4. Compliance & Legal Status

This is a non-commercial beta service. All users must comply with UAE Federal Law No. 34 of 2021 regarding Cybercrime. Violation results in immediate revocation of service access.
